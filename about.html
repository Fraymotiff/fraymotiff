About Lua Jaxuka



I'm Lua Jaxuka, also known as Fraymotiff. I am a Brasilian indigenous freelance illustrator and Youtuber located on capital area of Rio Grande do Sul, Brasil.

Games, Japanese pop culture and nature are my biggest inspirations and that My work is influenced by both a lot of pop culture and my own ancestral roots, and as the years progress that becomes even more evident. I like to explore with a lot of mediums, not limitating myself to a singular type of artistry. I am very aware and curious about the directions that I can take with my work. My style also changes a good amount, depending on the medium i am working with, I am capable of doing both cartooon and realism (although having realism as style of preference). I am very invested in character design and storybuilding, and have been working on my own comic for quite some time in 2022.

I have worked in many private commissions and have sold my character designs previously and it is something that I have great passion for! I also am an avid cosplayer and propmaker, having worked in the field of propmaking for 2 years in my teenage years. I am a bachelor graduate on the Visual Arts College on UFRGS (Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul), and as of now, (march 2023) am 3 semesters away from graduating and working on my BFA. I have always had an inclinations for video making, i love editing and recording, especially my process.
